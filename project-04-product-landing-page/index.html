MGear Apparel at a Glance

MGear Apparel at a Glance Established in the fall of 2014, the MGear Apparel line by the Megabucks Amateur Pool League was designed with the billiard and pool enthusiasts in mind. Our goal was to make a product that would appeal to the pool community and anyone who appreciated fashion, art, and style.

Our Mission: To Be The Best Cue Sports Apparel Provider in The U.S.

Like many other athletes, those who compete in cue sports never settle for average. And neither do we. We have a mission that’s as clear: To be the best cue sports apparel brand in the U.S. Every day begins with the goal to research, create and sell the best products possible and to offer a customer service experience that’s unmatched by our competitors.

Our Attitude

We are only a clothing line, but we believe we can be so much more through the products we provide. We want our products to provide comfort and instill confidence, to inspire, and to initiate competition. We want our customers to see value in all their acquisitions from us and grow to appreciate that MGear Apparel is not only a clothing line but also an entity that can help transform their lives positively.
